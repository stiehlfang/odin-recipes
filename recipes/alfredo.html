Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Rich and creamy alfredo that is simple to make, but is impressive enough for company.

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta.
  • Butter: 1 cup of butter.
  • Cream: 3/4 pint of heavy cream.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and garlic salt to taste.
  • Cheese: 3/4 cup Romano and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese.

Cooking steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated romano and parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
